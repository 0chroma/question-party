What is this?

Question Party was originally written by a guy named Will Carlough (in the video below). He made question party, but it was ugly and wasn't up all the time, so we redid it. Basically we just pull questions out of a set of RSS feeds, and present them to you to answer.

We are the Lucid Desktop team, a team of several people who write a web desktop. We did this to get our feet wet with Persevere before we started 2.0. We hope you enjoy Question Party!
